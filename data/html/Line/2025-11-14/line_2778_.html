Fintech Risk & Business Reporting Manager

BundangLINE Financial Corp.CorporateRisk ManagementFull-time
  • Share on Line
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on X
  • Share on LinkedIn
  • Copy Link

F Risk Management Team is a core department dedicated to identifying and managing all potential risk scenarios that LINE's financial products and its group companies may face. In the rapidly changing fintech industry, our top priority is to protect the company's growth and provide stable, reliable services.

We are looking for a proactive and detail-oriented professional to join our Fintech Risk Management and Business Management role. This unique dual role will be responsible for both identifying and mitigating risks to our financial products and services, as well as providing critical business analysis and reporting to senior leadership. You will act as the "go-to" person for all risks that may affect our fintech offerings, working cross-functionally with product, legal and corporate affairs teams to develop innovative solutions and prevent disruptions. Additionally, you will play a key role in the company's corporate planning by managing key performance indicators (KPIs), analyzing our platform's business models, and preparing reports for executives.

Job Responsibilities (담당업무)

  • Identify, measure, analyze, control and mitigate operational and business risks
  • Lead cross-functional departments implementing contingency plans for all risks relating to LINE’s products and services
  • Establish and maintain risk management policy, system and procedure
  • Educate stakeholders on operational and business risk management
  • Monitor regulatory changes and/or geopolitical development and flag any possible risks

Qualifications (자격요건)

  • Minimum 2 years of experience in financial industry/sector mainly focused on operational risk and/or overall business risk
  • Bachelor or Master degree in Business Administration, Finance, Legal or equivalent practical experience
  • Excellent English verbal and written communication skills
  • Knowledge of global fintech industry, including policy and regulations
  • Good organizational skills and the ability to work effectively with supervision
  • Flexibility for international travel and overseas work

Preferred Qualifications (우대사항)

  • Fluent language skill in Japanese is a plus

전형안내

  • 서류 전형 > 1차 면접 > 2차 면접 > 3차 면접 > 레퍼런스 체크 및 처우 협의 > 최종 합격 > 입사
     - 전형은 상황 및 일정에 따라 변경될 수 있습니다.
     - 2차 면접 중 영어 인터뷰가 진행될 예정입니다.
