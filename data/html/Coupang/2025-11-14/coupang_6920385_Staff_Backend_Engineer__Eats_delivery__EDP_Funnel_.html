Staff Backend Engineer (Eats delivery, EDP Funnel)

회사 소개

쿠팡이츠는 고객에게 음식을 배달해주는 서비스입니다. 여전히 빠르게 성장중인 비즈니스이며, 조직의 성장과 함께 할 개발자를 찾습니다. Eats Delivery Engineering 조직은 배달원들의 효율적인 업무를 위해 배달원의 계정 시스템부터 실시간 배송 관리까지 음식배달에 관여하는 시스템을 구축, 운영하고 있습니다.

Eats Delivery 서비스 백엔드 개발자

다양한 기술 스택을 활용한 개발 (Cloud Native, MSA, Docker, Kafka, Redis, MySQL, etc)

최상의 배송 경험을 제공하기 위한 높은 시스템 신뢰성

서울, 상하이, 베이징, 샌프란시스코에 걸쳐 협업 필요

Java 또는 그 외 객체 지향 프로그래밍 언어로 코딩한 7년 이상의 경험

계정 시스템 개발 경험

Coupang Eats is a service that delivers food to customers. We are still a rapidly growing business and are looking for a development leader to join us as we grow.

The Eats Delivery Engineering organization builds and operates systems related to food delivery, from delivery account systems to real-time delivery management, for efficient work by delivery riders.

  • Eats Delivery Service Developer
  • Development using various technology stacks (Cloud Native, MSA, Docker, Kafka, Redis, MySQL, etc)
  • High system reliability to provide the best delivery experience
  • Collaboration is required across Seoul, Shanghai, Beijing, and San Francisco

Our vision is to build a trusted food delivery platform with a highly productive engineering team focused on providing customers with a reliable and easy delivery experience.

[Responsibilities]

  • Write testable, efficient, and well-designed code using software development best practices.
  • Collect and improve specifications and requirements according to technical requirements
  • Designing scalable architecture and systems and implementing products from the beginning
  • Responsible for code review/design review committee to raise standards of engineering excellence
  • Serves as a key engineer making decisions about system design
  • Keep abreast of the latest technology/industry trends and apply them to operations and operations.

[We are looking for]

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in computer science or related field
  • More than 7 years of experience in large-scale distributed systems, system design, and algorithms.
  • 5+ years of experience coding in Java or another object-oriented programming language.
  • Experience with object-oriented programming and proficiency in Java or other languages.
  • Experience in designing, building, and operating large-scale services
  • Proficient in using AWS and Spring framework.

[Preferred]

  • Experience working in online service industries such as e-commerce and O2O
  • Ability to handle multiple competing priorities in a rapidly changing environment
  • Expertise in leading delivery logistics of large-scale services for complex business offerings
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills in English.
  • Location and logistics-related development experience.
  • Account/Member service experience.

[전형 절차]

  • 서류전형 > 1차 인터뷰 > 최종 인터뷰
  • 전형 절차는 직무별로 다르게 운영될 수 있으며, 일정 및 상황에 따라 변동될 수 있습니다.
  • 전형 일정 및 결과는 지원서에 등록하신 이메일로 개별 안내드립니다.

[참고 사항]

  • 본 공고는 모집 완료 시 조기 마감될 수 있습니다.
  • 지원서 내용 중 허위사실이 있는 경우에는 합격이 취소될 수 있습니다.
  • 보훈대상자 및 장애인 여부는 채용 과정에서 어떠한 불이익도 미치지 않습니다.
